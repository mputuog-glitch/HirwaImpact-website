HIRWAIMPACT

HELPING A CHILD HAVE A BRIGHTER FUTURE

HirwaImpact is a non-profit organization based in Rwanda, dedicated to breaking the cycle of poverty by providing holistic support to vulnerable children and their families.

Get Involved Learn More

Our Mission & Vision

Mission: HirwaImpact exists to secure a prosperous future for vulnerable children by providing for their essential needs and empowering their families to thrive.

Vision: We envision a world where every child, regardless of their origin, has the opportunity to grow, learn, and prosper in a safe and loving environment.

Holistic Care

We address the whole child—mind, body, and spirit—and the ecosystem of their family.

Dignity & Respect

We serve with profound respect for the dignity of every child and family in our program.

Sustainability

We don't just give aid; we build capacity. Our goal is family self-sufficiency.

Our Impact

HirwaImpact employs a unique, two-generation model that supports both the child and the parent, creating a stable foundation for lasting change.

Love Ministry

Charity Work & Discipleship

Our Love Ministry extends the heart of Christ through practical acts of charity and intentional discipleship. We believe that true transformation happens when we meet both physical and spiritual needs.

Through this ministry, we provide food, clothing, shelter, and medical care to those in need, demonstrating God's love in tangible ways. But we don't stop there - we walk alongside individuals and families, sharing the hope found in Jesus Christ.

"Suppose a brother or a sister is without clothes and daily food. If one of you says to them, 'Go in peace; keep warm and well fed,' but does nothing about their physical needs, what good is it? In the same way, faith by itself, if it is not accompanied by action, is dead." James 2:15-17

Raising Tomorrow's Church Leaders

We focus on teaching children the full gospel to secure tomorrow's church. We believe that a well-raised child in the ways of the Lord becomes a strong, faithful leader for the future church.

Our approach includes:

  • Biblical literacy programs that help children understand God's Word
  • Character development based on Christian values
  • Mentorship relationships with mature believers
  • Practical ministry opportunities to develop leadership skills
  • Teaching on the importance of prayer and dependence on God

By investing in children today, we are building the foundation for a strong, vibrant church tomorrow. These children will become the pastors, evangelists, teachers, and faithful servants who will carry the gospel to the next generation.

"Train up a child in the way he should go, and when he is old he will not depart from it." Proverbs 22:6

Soul Voice Ministry

Worship in Truth and Spirit

Soul Voice Ministry is dedicated to teaching children and youth how to worship God in both truth and spirit. We believe that worship is not just about singing songs but about connecting with God authentically from the heart.

Through this ministry, we explore:

Biblical Foundation

Understanding what Scripture says about true worship

Heart Posture

Developing a genuine heart of worship beyond external expressions

Community Worship

Learning to worship together as the body of Christ

Musical Expression

Developing musical skills to glorify God with excellence

Our gatherings include times of prayer, Bible study, worship through music, and teaching on various aspects of worship. We create a safe space where children and youth can explore their God-given gifts and use them to honor the Lord.

"Yet a time is coming and has now come when the true worshipers will worship the Father in the Spirit and in truth, for they are the kind of worshipers the Father seeks. God is spirit, and his worshipers must worship in the Spirit and in truth." John 4:23-24

Our Founder

Founder's Image

NDIZIHIWE HIRWA WESLEY

Born into a devout Christian family in Rwanda, NDIZIHIWE HIRWA WESLEY developed a deep compassion for vulnerable children from an early age. Growing up, he witnessed firsthand the struggles faced by children in his community who lacked access to basic necessities like education, shelter, and healthcare.

His Christian upbringing instilled in him the values of service, compassion, and the belief that every child is created with purpose and potential. After completing his education, Wesley felt a divine calling to address the systemic issues keeping children in poverty.

"Every child deserves the opportunity to fulfill their God-given potential. It is our privilege to walk alongside them, removing barriers and nurturing dreams."

In 2022, after years of volunteering with local community organizations, Wesley established HirwaImpact with a vision to create holistic change. The name "Hirwa" meaning "Prosperity" in Kinyarwanda, reflects his commitment to helping children and families not just survive, but thrive.

Under Wesley's leadership, HirwaImpact has grown from a small initiative helping a handful of children to an organization making significant impact across multiple communities. His unique approach of supporting both children and their parents has proven effective in creating sustainable change.
